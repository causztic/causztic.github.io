Can't believe I procrastinated another week before deciding to post the second day! (I've also discovered I deleted the notes I created when we were in Japan. Relying on memory now..)

For our first day we decided to grab a simple breakfast from the Fresco store nearby and walk to Kyomizu-dera temple, as it was just nearby where we stayed.

 

 

Afterwards, we headed towards Uji, which was famous for their tea. We went to Nakamura Tokichi Honten, and was greeted by a long queue. We wrote our names on a list and waited for our turn.. (I wrote my name as Yu-sama hahaha)

There were hot coals burning outside for the customers to keep themselves warm while waiting, and outdoor radiators too!
